Do’s
Think before you post — whatever you upload can stay online permanently.
Keep your profile clean and professional, especially on platforms like LinkedIn.
Use strong and unique passwords to protect your accounts.
Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading fake news.
Be respectful while commenting or messaging others online.


Don’ts
Don’t share personal details like your phone number, address, or passwords publicly.
Don’t click on suspicious links or messages from unknown sources.
Don’t post offensive, abusive, or inappropriate content.
Don’t blindly trust everything you see online.
Don’t overshare your daily activities or location publicly.
