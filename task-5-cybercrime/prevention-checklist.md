Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist
Never share your OTP, PIN, or bank details with anyone, even if they claim to be from the bank or customer support.
Always double-check before clicking on any links sent through SMS, WhatsApp, or email, especially if they seem urgent or suspicious.
Make sure you are using only official and trusted apps for online payments and banking.
Keep your phone and apps updated to avoid security vulnerabilities.
Enable app lock or biometric security for payment apps to add an extra layer of protection.
Avoid using public Wi-Fi while making online payments or accessing sensitive accounts.
Regularly check your bank statements and transaction history to spot any unusual activity early.
Create strong and unique passwords for your accounts instead of using the same password everywhere.



UPI Safety Tips-
Never approve a payment request unless you are sure you are sending money to the correct person.
Always verify the receiver’s name before confirming any UPI transaction.



Report Cybercrime-
If you ever become a victim of online fraud, report it immediately:
National Cyber Crime Portal: cybercrime.gov.in
